About Us

Fable Bakery is a small vegan home-based bakery located on Barry Island. We specialise in cakes and sweet breads. We also offer a range of gluten-free products. All our products are prepared in a vegan kitchen.

For large events such as weddings and parties, please provide reasonable notice by contacting us via email or social media.
